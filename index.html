Team

As a small group of blockchain enthusiasts and entrepreneurs we started investing in cryptocurrencies in 2012. Oncapital fund started functioning in 2016. The company is a private fund focused exclusively on investing in cryptocurrencies, tokens and blockchain startups in their early stages.

Our mission is to support the development of blockchain technology through investments and consultations. We invest in companies with innovative technologies and business models based on digital assets.

Thanks to our 10 years’ experience in online business and 6 years’ experience in crypto asset management, we have a rich knowledge and understanding about how a startup becomes a real business.

Tran is the founder and investment manager of Oncapital. Blockchain enthusiast and entepreneur. He has 10 years’ experience in software development and an online business. CEO of EduNet Company.

«I believe that the blockchain technology will become widely accepted and investments will bring real outcome for the creation of the infrastructure necessary for mass adoption».
